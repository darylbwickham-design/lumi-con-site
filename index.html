About LumiCon

LumiCon is a modular open source stream controller platform built to stay flexible, easy to set up, and affordable. The Matrix Mini combines a compact 6x6 keypad with a smart LCD and pet module, giving you a polished controller on the desk now and a platform that can grow with future LumiCon hardware later.

It is designed for both free and premium Lumia Stream users. Short and long key actions, custom messages back to the unit, and room for deeper integrations all sit underneath a simple setup path.
