Before you flash

  1. Use a USB data cable, not a charge-only cable.
  2. Close Arduino Serial Monitor and anything else that may be holding the serial port hostage.
  3. When the browser asks for a serial device, choose the LumiCon port.
  4. If you are recovering a board, use Recent unless you specifically need an older build.
